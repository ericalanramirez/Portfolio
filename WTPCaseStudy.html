Welcome to Where's The Party! The easiest way to find your next good time!

THE PROBLEM

born out of need

Speed, simplicity, access and ease of use are all highly valued commodities in today's busy world. So is having a good time! But, finding where to go, what to do can be cumbersome. Having to depend on various searches, social media and word of mouth isn't simple or fast. As a member of the LGBTQ community, I myself often find myself looking for events, parties and noticed that my friends did as well. We all recognized the need for something better, simpler and faster than how we have been searching for parties and events.

THE SOLUTION

problem solved

Where's The Party was born and geared to be a one stop shop for finding LGBTQ parties and events. A place to view event listings, photos, locations and even be able to read and write reviews.

User Surveys

survey says...

The user survey really focused on how people search for events; what they like and don't like about that process. The majorty found events on Facebook with the Scruff app and Gaycities app coming in second and third respectively. Overwhelmingly, many found the process of looking for parties and events, long, tideous, difficult, lacking sufficient details and agreed that a one stop shop for all of this information would be useful.

BRANDING & STYLE GUIDE

present yourself to the world

The Rainbow is known worldwide as an LGBTQ icon and data was collected that while it doesn't have to overwhelm a design, a nice "touch" is appreciated and recognized.

The idea of this app is to eventually highlight parties and events in different cities as it expands. As an avid traveler my mind went to luggage tags and I wanted to mimic that in my design by using the bold three letter city codes.

Finally, since the process of finding events and parties had been less than fun before, I chose a font that was carefree and fun for the main title. No matter where you are when you pull up this app, you know you're in for a good time!

CONCLUSION

long is the road from conception to completion

Working on this app was a long process but it gave me a new found appreciation for combining data, user wants and needs and delivering it all in a thoughtful and eyecatching design package that is functional and easy to use. I learned that regardless of any preconcieved notions, that data is king and will truthfuly guide a design in the right direction, but only if you follow it! Wheres the Party was merely an idea based out of a confirmed need. Through surveys, testing, sketching, wireframing and and prototyping (most times, more than once) it revealed itself and became reality. The answers were all there, it was just my job to pay attention, collect and arrange them to create something tangilbe that others can use.
